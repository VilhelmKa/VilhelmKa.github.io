Introduction

Welcome to Uzel's MCC simulator! This is a tool designed to help you simulate Minecraft Championship events and predict the outcome based on player statistics.

Using this simulator, you can select teams, choose mini-games, and simulate events to see which team comes out on top.

Whether you're a fan of MCC or just looking for a fun way to test your predictions, Uzel's MCC simulator is the perfect tool for you!
